Hodor Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-06-12

Overview

This privacy policy applies to the Android application Hodor available on the Google Play Store, and is published under the Lingmates developer account.

No Personal Data Collected

  • No user account creation or login is required
  • Hodor does not collect, use, or share any personal or sensitive user data
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are integrated
  • Hodor does not access device identifiers, contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • There is no data transmission to our servers or third parties

Data Security

As no personal or sensitive data is accessed, collected, or stored by the Hodor app, there are no data security risks or handling processes in place. The app functions fully offline and does not interact with external services.

Data Retention and Deletion

Hodor does not store any user data, either locally or remotely. As such, there is no data retention, and no user data is subject to deletion. Uninstalling the app fully removes all locally cached configuration and temporary files.

Legal Basis

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws, since Hodor does not collect or process personal data, no user consent, data processing agreements, or user access mechanisms are required.

Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change in the future, we will update this privacy policy accordingly and ensure full compliance with Google Play's requirements and global privacy regulations.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, you may contact us at: privacy+hodor@lingmates.com
