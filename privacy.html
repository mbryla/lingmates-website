Lingmates Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-06-01

Overview

This privacy policy applies to the website lingmates.com and any apps published under our name on the Google Play Store.

No Personal Data Collected

  • No account creation or login is required
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) are integrated
  • No contact forms, comments, or subscriptions are present
  • No data is transmitted to us or any third party

Apps on Google Play

Our apps on the Google Play Store follow the same principle: they do not collect or share any user data. We do not request or access any personal information, device identifiers, or permissions that involve data processing.

Legal Basis

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), since we do not collect or process personal data, there is no need for user consent, data processing agreements, or data access mechanisms.

Changes to This Policy

If our practices change and we begin to collect or use data in the future, we will update this privacy policy accordingly and ensure full compliance with GDPR and other applicable data protection laws.
